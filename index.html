Teaching notes

  • 1. Tax shields lower the effective cost of debt.
  • 2. Higher leverage raises equity beta, so Re rises faster than Rd falls.
  • 3. Distress costs create an interior optimum instead of “more debt is always better,” which would make class discussion embarrassingly short.

Decision rule

In this model, the preferred leverage is the debt ratio that minimizes WACC. For a real transaction, pair this with covenants, refinancing risk, and peer comparables.
